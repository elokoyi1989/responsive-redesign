Key issues identified in the original Website:

  • Efficiency: No search function; all of the clickable links are laid out unorganized
  • Accessibility gaps via WebAim WAV: This tool noted the lack of ARIA labels which stands out as a huge accessibility oversight. However, there are no images on the page, so maybe this choice isn’t as problematic as it could be on a different website.
  • Non-responsive layout: The two-column design breaks on narrow viewports, forcing horizontal scroll.
  • Lack of visual hierarchy: All text and links look identical. There is no built in opportunity to intuitively learn where certain things might lead or where to find something important.
